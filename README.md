## Simple Encryptor Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 7, 2025

We take your privacy extremely seriously. Our application, Simple Encryptor, was built from the ground up to ensure the maximum security of your data. Our policy is simple:

**We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. Ever.**

### 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Simple Encryptor is a 100% offline application. We do not have access to, cannot see, and do not collect:
* The text you enter for encryption or decryption.
* Your encryption keys.
* Your passwords (neither the Main nor the Decoy password).
* Any other personal information.

### 2. How Your Data is Stored

All data you generate within the app (including your saved keys and password hashes) is stored **exclusively on your device**.

This data is saved in a local, encrypted storage (`EncryptedSharedPreferences`) on your phone. Only you, with your app password, can access it. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all this information is permanently deleted.

**We (the developers) have no access to these files.**

### 3. Biometric (Fingerprint) Login

When you use the fingerprint login feature, the authentication process is managed entirely by the Android Operating System. Our application **does not get access** to your actual biometric data (your fingerprint). We only receive a "Yes" (success) or "No" (failure) response from the OS.

### 4. Root Detection

The application performs a local check to see if your device is rooted. This check is used **only** for security purposes (to trigger the sabotage mechanism if detected). The result of this check is never transmitted anywhere.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Since we do not collect any data, we do not expect this policy to change. However, if we ever update it, we will notify you via this website.
